## Design Guidelines

### Colors:
- Primary: #2e7d32 (Green)
- Secondary: #558b2f (Dark Green)
- Accent: #ff6f00 (Orange)
- Background: White with subtle gradient

### Fonts:
- Headings: Bold, Sans-serif (Calibri/Arial)
- Body: Regular, Sans-serif
- Hindi text: Nirmala UI or Mangal

### Images:
- Use high-quality photos of farmers
- Icons for features (consistent style)
- Screenshots of actual interface
- Avoid clip art, use authentic imagery

### Layout:
- Minimal text per slide (5-7 bullets max)
- Large fonts (min 24pt for body)
- White space is good
- One main idea per slide
